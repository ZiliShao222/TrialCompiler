# Protocol Synopsis

This synthetic Phase II study evaluates investigational product TC-001 in adults aged 18 to 65 years. Approximately 120 participants will be randomized in a 1:1 ratio to TC-001 or placebo.

The primary objective is to evaluate whether TC-001 improves the Symptom Severity Score compared with placebo. The primary endpoint is the change from baseline in Symptom Severity Score at Week 12.

Participants will attend screening, baseline, Week 4, Week 8, Week 12 and follow-up visits. The Week 12 visit is the main efficacy assessment visit for the primary endpoint.

Safety assessments include adverse events, laboratory tests and vital signs throughout the study.
