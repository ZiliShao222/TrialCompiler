# Statistical Principles

The primary efficacy analysis will compare the change from baseline in Symptom Severity Score at Week 12 between treatment groups.

The full analysis set includes all randomized participants who receive at least one dose of study treatment. The safety set includes all participants who receive at least one dose of study treatment and have at least one post-baseline safety assessment.

The primary endpoint will be analyzed using an analysis of covariance model with treatment group and baseline score as covariates.
