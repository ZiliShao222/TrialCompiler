# Objectives and Endpoints

## 2.1 Primary Objective

To assess the effect of TC-001 compared with placebo on symptom improvement.

## 2.2 Primary Endpoint

The primary endpoint is the change from baseline in Symptom Severity Score at Week 12.

## 2.3 Secondary Endpoints

Secondary endpoints include change from baseline in quality-of-life score at Week 12 and responder rate at Week 12.
