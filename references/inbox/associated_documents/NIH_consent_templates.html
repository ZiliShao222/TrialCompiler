Consent Templates

Below are all of the NIH Consent Templates. These templates should be used when writing a new NIH Intramural Program informed consent form. Please refer to specific pages under Consent Development for more information about each template.

Consent Templates and Guidance

These templates are intended to help investigators construct documents that are as short as possible and written in plain language. The informed consent form (ICF) templates provided by the IRB comply with federal regulations.
