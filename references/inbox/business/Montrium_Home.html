Frequently asked questions

Find answers about Montrium product and services, implementation, and how it works for your organization.

Is Montrium's software validated for GxP environments?

Yes. Montrium products are built for GxP compliance and align with FDA 21 CFR Part 11, EudraLex Volume 4 Annex 11, and GDPR. A validation package is included with implementation to reduce your internal validation burden.

What support is included after go-live?

Every customer works with a named Product Specialist and Customer Success Manager after implementation. A Help Centre includes product videos, articles, and an in-system Q&A chat. Support is available for platform issues throughout your subscription.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation typically takes 4-8 weeks, depending on organization size, number of active studies, and the volume of documents to migrate. Customers with critical timelines have gone live in as little as 3 weeks.

Where is customer data hosted?

All customer data is hosted on Microsoft Azure. Data is isolated per customer, encrypted in transit and at rest, and continuously monitored. Montrium has completed a SOC 2 Type I audit.
