Vision

To make clinical trial information instantly clear, actionable, and secure, transforming complex protocols into usable insights in minutes. Built for speed, clarity, and regulatory confidence.

About us

Clinials is the AI platform built to make clinical trial content usable, without compromising compliance, security, or scientific integrity.

Clinical trials generate some of the most complex documentation in healthcare. Protocols run hundreds of pages. Stakeholders are overloaded. Decisions slow down not because the science is unclear, but because the information is inaccessible.

Clinials exists to fix that.

We transform dense clinical trial documents into clear, structured, and purpose-built summaries that support faster approvals, better decisions, and more confident communication across the trial lifecycle.

What we do!

Clinials uses AI to extract what actually matters from clinical trial protocols and related documents, and presents it in formats tailored to how different stakeholders work.

We generate:

  • Plain language protocol synopses

  • Operational and feasibility summaries

  • Compliance-ready reports

  • Simplified content for internal teams, sites, regulators, and investors

The result: less time interpreting documents, fewer misunderstandings, and faster progress from planning to execution.

Built for a Regulated Industry — Not Repurposed From Another One

Clinials is not a generic AI tool adapted for life sciences.

Our platform is engineered specifically for clinical trials, with workflows designed for sponsors, CROs, sites, consultants, and regulatory teams. Every output is grounded in source documentation, traceable, and designed to support real-world trial operations.

Unlike most AI solutions, Clinials operates in a fully closed environment.
Your data is never shared with external large language models.

No training on your content.
No leakage.
No ambiguity about where your data goes.

Security and control are not optional in clinical research, they are foundational.

Our Perspective

Founded in 2021, Clinials was created in response to a growing gap in clinical research:
more data, more documentation, and more pressure, but no better way to communicate what matters.

We were the first to pioneer AI-generated plain language protocol synopses designed specifically for clinical trials. Since then, we’ve focused relentlessly on one goal: making clinical trial information clearer, faster to use, and fit for the realities of modern research.

AI doesn’t replace expertise in clinical trials.
It removes friction, so expertise can actually scale.
