Literature Research Takes Months

Finding and reviewing relevant trials to support protocol design takes 2-3 months of searching.

Hard to Compare Similar Trials

Manually extracting study designs, endpoints, and methods from dozens of trials is time-consuming.

Writing Protocol Sections is Slow

Creating background sections, study rationale, and methodology descriptions requires extensive literature review and citations.

Missing Important Studies

Manual searches miss relevant trials that could inform better design decisions or safety considerations.

Inconsistent Design Choices

Without comprehensive evidence review, justifying design decisions like endpoints or criteria is difficult.
