In order to share, you must first protect

Shield structured and unstructured information

ANONYMIZE® employs powerful machine learning to enable you to efficiently redact or anonymize unstructured clinical documents, structured datasets, and medical images, including Microsoft Office files, emails, CSV, SAS, XPT, and PDF formats.

Use ANONYMIZE® to quantify the risk of re-identification and generate risk assessment reports. A centralized interface provides a complete view of all projects and tasks, helping you anonymize data and documents together while saving time.

Maximize data utility

With ANONYMIZE®, you can maximize and maintain the integrity of study data and enable secondary analysis, while applying a range of information-protection measures. ANONYMIZE supports rule-based anonymization decisions and provides visual feedback to track progress. Capabilities include:

  • Scramble patient IDs,
  • Mask private company and patient information,
  • Change ages to age ranges,
  • Engage full or pseudo anonymization, and more!

Meet new compliance requirements

For years, many Sponsors did not post redacted or anonymized trial reports, but new regulations are increasing these transparency demands. Unfortunately, manual redaction of Adobe PDF, Microsoft Word, and other files is difficult and time consuming.

Now ANONYMIZE® automates the process, redacting and/or anonymizing information at the same time in the same document, covering:

  • Personal protected information (PPI)
  • Personal protected data (PPD)
  • Commercial confidential information (CCI)
